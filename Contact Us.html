Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 10 AM to 10 PM, seven days a week. Our kitchen closes at 9:30 PM.

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations through our website or by phone. We recommend making reservations in advance, especially for weekends.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a parking lot available for our customers. Street parking is also available nearby..

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering services for events and special occasions. Please contact us for more information and pricing.
